Scholastica provide beauty services , she sometimes does my nails and during the session we discussed her business needs ,and she once mentioned that she would like a website for her salon, which inspired me to choose her for this project

Scholastica owns Milano_beauty Salon located on Kimathi Street, Lyric House, 2nd Floor in Nairobi. She offers services including nails, eyelash extensions, hair styling, massage therapy, waxing, and pedicure/manicure services

Her target audience is women looking for professional, affordable, and luxurious beauty services even men sometimes but  she provides sevices for women mostly . She prefers a feminine and elegant website design using brown and soft colors to reflect her brand identity 

The main goal of the website is to showcase her services and make it easy for clients , especially new customers to contact her via phone or WhatsApp for bookings. The website should feel welcoming, clean, and visually appealing.
